# Spontana — Brand Soul

## What is Spontana?

Spontana is an event aggregator for Stockholm — built for people who actually live here.
Not tourists. Not people who plan two weeks in advance.
People who check their phone at 6 PM and want to know what's happening tonight.

It's the city, surfaced. The good stuff, filtered in. The boring stuff, left out.

---

## The Name

*Spontana* — Swedish-flavored spontaneity.
It implies: last-minute, impulsive, alive. Going somewhere because you felt like it.
The app should feel like a friend texting you "there's something on tonight, you in?"

---

## Visual Identity

Dark. Neon. Electric.
Stockholm at night, rendered in glitch and glow.
Cyberpunk aesthetic — but not try-hard. Cool, not cosplay.

Think: wet cobblestones reflecting purple streetlights.
Not: sci-fi movie poster.

---

## The User

**Age:** 20–35. Stockholmer. Has probably lived here a few years.
**Attitude:** Curious but jaded. Knows the city has layers most people miss.
**Motivation:** Wants to do something real — not the obvious tourist pick, not nothing.
**Behavior:** Decides last-minute. Scrolls for 3 minutes max. Either goes or doesn't.

They don't need to be sold to. They just need to be shown.

---

## Voice & Tone

**Dry. Witty. Understated.**

Scandinavian deadpan energy. The humor is there if you look for it — not announced.
No exclamation points trying too hard. No corporate enthusiasm.
No "Join us for an unforgettable evening of..."

Say more with less. Trust the reader.

### Tone examples

| Instead of this | Say this |
|---|---|
| "Don't miss this incredible event!" | "This is on. You probably should go." |
| "Join us for a night of live music and fun!" | "Live music. Bar. Obvious reasons to show up." |
| "We couldn't find any events matching your search." | "Nothing here. Try loosening the filter — Stockholm is hiding something." |
| "Welcome to Spontana! Discover events near you." | "Something's happening tonight. Here's what." |
| "Error loading events. Please try again." | "Couldn't reach the grid. Try again." |

### Rules

- **No fluff.** Cut the sentence in half. Then cut it again.
- **No hype punctuation.** Enthusiasm is implied, not performed.
- **Light irony is fine.** Heavy sarcasm isn't.
- **Stockholm-aware.** It's okay to be specific — neighborhoods, references, weather.
- **Don't explain the joke.** If a line is clever, leave it alone.

---

## What Spontana Is Not

- Not a tourist guide
- Not a corporate events platform
- Not trying to be Eventbrite or Meetup
- Not neutral or generic — it has a point of view

---

## The Feel

If Spontana were a person, they'd:
- Know the good bar that isn't on any list
- Text you at 7 PM with a show that starts at 9
- Not make a big deal out of it
- Be right, though

---

## Writing Checklist

Before publishing any copy, ask:

1. Would a 28-year-old Stockholmer roll their eyes at this?
2. Is there a word that could be cut?
3. Does it sound like a human or a marketing email?
4. Is it trying too hard to be cool? (If yes: dial back.)
5. Does it fit the dark, electric, slightly-wry world of Spontana?
